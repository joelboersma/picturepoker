Rules

Picture Poker is a player vs. dealer card game.

At the beginning of each round, you and the dealer are each dealt five cards. From here, try to get the best poker hand that you can by discarding cards you don't want. You can select which cards that you want to discard by clicking on them. Then, push "Draw" to draw new cards to replace them.

If you do not want to discard any cards, don't select any cards, and push the "Hold" button.

Once the dealer discards their selected cards, everyone reveals their hands. Whoever has the best hand wins!

You can also bet gems for a chance at a profit. You can bet up to 5 gems at the start of a round, and if you win, you will get multiple times what you spent. Your payout multiplier depends on how good of a hand you win with.

Best of luck, and have fun!
