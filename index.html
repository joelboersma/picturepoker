Picture Poker

Picture Poker is a player vs. dealer card game.

At the beginning of each round, you and the dealer are each dealt five cards. Then you have a chance replace as many of your cards as you want to try to get a better hand. Choose cards to discard by clicking on them. You can also bet up to 5 gems each round for a chance at a profit.

Once the dealer does the same, you each reveal your cards. Whoever has the best hand wins! And if you win, you earn multiple times your money back. The better your hand, the higher the payout!

Best of luck, and have fun!
